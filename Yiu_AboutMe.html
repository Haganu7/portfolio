About Me

A bit about me and my experience with computer science


Hey, my name is Hagan Yiu and this is my website! Computer science has always interested me to some extent due to its reach into our every day lives as well as the possibilities it gives to the future of humanity. While I really do like computer science I'm not completely sure I want to make a career out of it. I'm still not sure what I want to do in the future but anything related to computer science is certainly an option. If I due end up pusuing this path I would want to create something new that I could show the world and maybe even make it better. I guess as any other human, I would like to leave my mark on the world in the time that I have.
If I have a specific goal in my mind that I am interested in I will dedicate a large amount of time into whatever that thing is until it is completed. I don't like leaving things unfinished and will try to finish it all at once. This is how I do most things like when I write essays, do homework, and reading. I also consider myself an analytical person. I often over think things and tend to stay on topics that have past. This helps me make big decisions but it also can be rather distracting. Whether of not you would consider this a positive trait is up to you.
If I'm being completely honest, so far my high school career has been rather uneventful and I have yet to really accomplish anything that is worth remembering. (Except maybe this website. Though it is simple I am still quite proud of it.) One thing about me is that I like games. Any type of game whether is be a video game, a sport, or even board games. From these games you can really learn a lot about team work, planning ahead, and how to adapt to new things. All these skills can be incorporated into computer science and I think they can help a lot.
